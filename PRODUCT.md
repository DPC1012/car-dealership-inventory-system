# Product Brief — DriveLine / Roadstead Motors

## Product Overview
DriveLine / Roadstead Motors is an award-winning luxury automotive marketplace and inventory system. It allows prospective buyers to search, inspect specs, and purchase premium vehicles in real-time with exact INR (`₹`) pricing, while providing administrators with a control suite to manage inventory stock, restock vehicles, upload high-definition vehicle photography via ImageKit, and track availability.

## Target Audience & Aesthetic POV
- **Audience:** Luxury car buyers, automotive collectors, and dealership inventory managers.
- **Visual POV:** "The Showroom Floor" after closing — halogen amber accents on deep graphite and warm off-white, large high-contrast automotive photography, rounded cards, floating glassmorphic search panels, soft elevation, and Apple-like minimalist layout.

## Surface Modes
- **Marketplace Dashboard (`frontend/src/App.tsx`):** Mode: **Persuade & Operate** — High-impact hero banner with floating quick-search panel, category browser, brand showcase, feature highlights, vehicle grid with dynamic stock indicators (`IN STOCK`, `LOW STOCK`, `SOLD OUT`), and administrative management suite.
